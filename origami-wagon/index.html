The OpportunityProblem & Insight

⚠️ Problem

Today’s folding wagons are homogeneous — tube frame plus fabric body. They don’t store efficiently, deploy elegantly, or differentiate on the shelf.

  • Bulky collapsed profiles
  • Multi-step setup frustrates consumers
  • No meaningful innovation in a decade

💡 Insight

Living hinges + origami folding can combine basket and structure into a single sheet that folds flat — dramatically more compact than tube/fabric systems.

  • One piece replaces frame + fabric + fasteners
  • Intuitive fold/unfold in seconds
  • Elegant, differentiated form factor

Core TechnologyLiving Hinge & Origami Folding

🔗 Living Hinges

Thin, flexible sections molded directly into the polymer sheet. No separate fasteners or hardware — the material itself is the mechanism.

📐 Origami Principles

Mountain and valley folds transform a flat sheet into a 3D structure. Crease patterns are engineered for exactly one stable deployed state.

🏗️ Structural Rigidity

Folded panels create double-wall sections that resist lateral loads. Corner geometry locks the basket open under load without clasps.

⚡ Key Advantages

  • No assembly — unfold and go
  • Fewer parts = lower BOM cost
  • Flat-pack shipping & retail display

Why SRPP?

  • Lighter than water — ultralow density
  • Excellent impact & puncture resistance
  • Ideal living-hinge fatigue life (PP family)
  • 100% recyclable (mono-material)
  • Thermoformable for complex crease patterns

AssessmentRisk & Payoff

⚠️ Key Risks

  • Folding mechanism may not function reliably at full wagon scale
  • Hinge fatigue over hundreds of fold cycles needs validation
  • Tooling investment for thermoformed SRPP sheet is significant
  • Consumer perception — polymer body vs. traditional fabric

🏆 Potential Payoff

  • Establishes an entirely new product category
  • No immediate competition — first-mover advantage
  • Strong IP potential (utility + design patents)
  • Lower landed cost at scale (fewer components)
  • Premium positioning with sustainability story
