Timber Ridge · Positioning

Timber Ridge Positioning

Timber Ridge is built to help consumers Outdoor Your Way™, serving life from the backyard to the backcountry with versatile, durable gear that makes more outdoor moments possible.

Competitive intent

Life is about the journey, the experience, and the memories made along the way. Timber Ridge is built to show up for every moment and every adventure.

What supports that position

We craft outdoor gear that redefines versatility, innovation, and durability so consumers can move confidently across backyard, sideline, campsite, and backcountry use.

Key takeaway

Timber Ridge is confident, versatile, innovative, authentic, fearless, and resilient, with a tone that is approachable without losing performance credibility.

Why it matters

This gives Timber Ridge a broader role than a single-category camp brand, with the flexibility to serve multiple outdoor occasions under one clear promise: Outdoor Your Way™.

Forge Creek · Positioning

Forge Creek Positioning

Forge Creek should be presented as Westfield’s hunting-focused brand build, grounded in authentic field credibility, disciplined assortment, and a tone that feels inside the category rather than adjacent to it.

Market role

Forge Creek is being built to speak directly to the hunting consumer with a tighter point of view, narrower field assortment, and more specialized brand posture.

Brand proof

The strongest signals today are tradition, camaraderie, and legacy, which give the brand credible hunting language even before scale.

Key takeaway

Forge Creek should be framed as a disciplined brand build in hunting, not as a website review or a broad outdoor experiment.

Why it matters

That sharper positioning gives Westfield a credible entry into hunting without diluting Timber Ridge’s broader outdoor role.

Forge Creek · Identity
Live brand cues

Forge Creek Identity

Forge Creek is being developed as a focused hunting brand with a darker visual tone, field-first assortment, and the kind of brand cues that signal category credibility from the inside out.

Tradition Camaraderie Legacy

Dark visual tone

The brand uses a darker, warmer field palette that immediately separates Forge Creek from broader outdoor lifestyle brands.

Focused assortment

The assortment is centered on purpose-built packs and hunting gear, keeping the brand specific rather than diffuse.

Field credibility

Tradition, camaraderie, and legacy are the strongest visible cues today and should anchor how we talk about the brand until the full brand book is in hand.
