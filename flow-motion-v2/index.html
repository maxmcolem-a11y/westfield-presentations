V2 angles the previously flat leaf spring by placing a wedge beneath it, creating two distinct fulcrum points with different force characteristics — easy lean-back, controlled return.V2 通过在原有水平板簧下方放置楔块使其倾斜，形成两个具有不同力学特性的支点 — 向后倾斜轻松，回位受控平稳。
🎯 Asymmetric Rocking
Rear fulcrum (fixed) = easy lean-back. Forward fulcrum (tunable) = controlled resistance. Natural, intuitive feel.后支点（固定）= 轻松后仰。前支点（可调）= 受控阻力。感觉自然，操作直觉。
🔧 Single-Variable Tuning
Move the forward fulcrum position to control rocking amplitude, force asymmetry, and pinch gap — all at once.移动前支点位置，可同时控制摇摆幅度、力学不对称性与夹缝间距。
🛡️ Pinch Point Elimination
Forward rock amplitude limited by fulcrum geometry — the front gap never closes enough to pinch.前倾幅度由支点几何结构限制 — 前侧间隙永远不会收窄至夹伤程度。

The Problem问题所在

V1 works — but can't be tuned.V1 可用——但无法调节。

The original Flow-Motion uses a flat leaf spring parallel to the ground. It delivers a satisfying rocking motion — but the symmetry creates three limitations that V2 directly solves.原始 Flow-Motion 采用平行于地面的平板弹簧，提供令人满意的摇动体验——但其对称性带来了三项局限，V2 直接解决了这些问题。
⚠️ Pinch Points夹伤隐患
Equal forward rock means the front gap closes under load — creating potential pinch hazards that require guards or design workarounds.等量前倾意味着在负载下前部间隙会闭合，产生潜在夹伤风险，需要防护装置或设计变通方案。
🔒 One-Size Rocking千篇一律的摇动感
Every chair model gets the same rocking feel. No way to tune for different seat heights, weight ranges, or use cases without redesigning the frame.每款椅型的摇动手感完全相同，无法针对不同座高、承重范围或使用场景进行调节，而无需重新设计框架。
👁️ Static Appearance静态外观
A flat spring parallel to the ground reads as inert.平行于地面的平板弹簧看起来毫无动感，无法在有人坐下之前传达出椅子可摇动的特性。 It doesn't communicate "this chair rocks" until someone sits in it.
V2 Solves All ThreeV2 一次性解决三大问题
Pinch points → Forward fulcrum geometry limits max deflection. Gap never closes enough to pinch.
One-size rocking → Swap the wedge to customize feel per model. Same frame, different personality.
Static look → Angled spring reads as motion at rest. Communicates "this rocks" before anyone sits.
Platform Advantage平台优势

The wedge is the personality.楔块即个性。

Same base frame tube and leaf spring across every Flow-Motion chair. The wedge component is the only variable — and it controls everything.所有 Flow-Motion 椅型共用同一基础框架管材和板簧，楔块组件是唯一的变量——却掌控一切。
🔧 Standardized Base标准化基础
Same frame tube + leaf spring across the entire lineup. Reduces tooling, simplifies manufacturing, lowers SKU complexity.整个产品线共用同一框架管材和板簧，减少模具投入，简化生产流程，降低 SKU 复杂度。
🎛️ Tunable Per Model按型号可调
Different wedge = different feel. A compact camp chair gets a different rocking profile than a tall director's chair. One tooling change, not a frame redesign.不同楔块 = 不同手感。紧凑型露营椅与高脚导演椅的摇动曲线各不相同，只需更换一套模具，无需重新设计框架。
💰 Low Tooling Cost模具成本低
Wedge is a simple injection molded or stamped component. Cheap to tool, cheap to modify, cheap to test multiple variants.楔块是简单的注塑或冲压件，模具成本低、修改成本低、多方案测试成本低。
🏪 Retail Flexibility零售灵活性
If a retailer wants a different "feel" for their exclusive — it's a wedge swap, not a frame change. Customize without multiplying tooling investment.
Business Model商业模式
Base frame, spring, and chair structure are shared infrastructure. The wedge is the personality — the cheapest, fastest component to change. One tuning cartridge per chair model across the entire Flow-Motion lineup.基础框架、弹簧与椅体结构为共享基础设施，楔块即个性——是最廉价、更换最快的零部件。整个 Flow-Motion 产品线每款椅型配备一个调节模块。
