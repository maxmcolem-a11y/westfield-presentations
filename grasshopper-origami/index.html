Why Grasshopper

  • Crease patterns are math. Kawasaki's theorem, Maekawa's theorem — directly expressible as node algorithms.
  • Iteration in seconds. "Pack under 60mm" = move one slider. All geometry updates live.
  • Physics built-in. Kangaroo 2 ships with Rhino 8. Watch a flat sheet fold to 3D in real time.
  • Fab outputs are parametric. The same definition that generates 3D form also produces DXF for laser cutting.
  • Free with Rhino 8. $995 one-time gets you everything: GH + Kangaroo + scripting.

Rhino 8 runs natively on Apple Silicon (M1–M4). Fast single-core CPU is exactly what Grasshopper's solver needs. 32 GB recommended for physics simulation.

Real-World Precedents

🛶

Oru Kayak / Oru Camp

Corrugated polypropylene sheet folded into a functional kayak — same material in the Oru Camp outdoor furniture line (PopLounge chair, SwitchTable). Folds to 5" profile. Direct competitive precedent.

👜

Issey Miyake

Bao Bao: triangular tile tessellation bag, variable volume. Pleats Please: heat-pleated polyester backpack, compresses to flat when empty. Proof of commercial viability at scale.

🪑

Nendo — Orizuru Series

Tokyo studio applies origami crease patterns directly to leather goods. Single flat sheet → finished bag with no main seam stitching. Computationally designed.

🚀

NASA JPL — Miura Solar Array

Rigid-panel Miura-fold solar array compresses to 1/10 deployed area. 1-DOF synchronous deployment — single pull deploys entire structure. Direct parallel for tent design. (NTRS 20140002353)

🔬

BYU Compliant Mechanisms Lab

SORCE pattern (Stacked Origami for Repeated Compression/Extension) for thick-panel folding. The "offset fold" technique used in Crane for aluminum panels is directly based on this research.

📐

Academic Foundation

ACM TOCHI 2023 (Crane paper) · ResearchGate rigid-origami + GH study · MDPI Sustainability Dec 2024 review of 174 origami furniture papers. Proven methodology, not experimental.
