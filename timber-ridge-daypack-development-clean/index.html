Volumetric control becomes the headline, not a hidden mechanism.

The technical story here is the semi-rigid top and bottom ring structure working with concertina side panels and an adjustable strap system that preserves force-free lid access at any compression state.

Ring architecture

Semi-rigid top and bottom rings stabilize the opening and preserve the body geometry through compression.

Concertina body

Side panels create a visible, mechanically legible method of volume reduction instead of relying on crushed fabric.

Mechanical advantage

The adjustable strap system permits lid access without fighting the compression state, which is the key differentiator.
