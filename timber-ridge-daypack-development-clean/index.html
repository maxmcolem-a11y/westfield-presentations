Diamond Final Result

The Diamond final design yields a highly structured silhouette with strong graphic potential.

Diamond and triangular panels, distinctive M-profile webbing, and a unique slanted zipper establish a strong brand identity through functional, rather than arbitrary, design elements.

Signature Read

Prominent triangular panels dictate the immediate structured silhouette.

Functional Geometry

The lower M-shape webbing projects its inner V upward to create the slanted zipper access point.

Utility Integration

Laptop storage and quick-access organization are embedded into the existing form to maintain architectural integrity.

06
Spiral Final Result

Hybrid sling and pack functionality, driven by a spiral panel architecture.

The panel flow bridges the gap between commuter access, ergonomic body wrap, and an athletic identity, strictly avoiding a tactical appearance.

Signature System

The spiral panels create a highly recognizable motion language visible from multiple angles.

Wearability Bridge

An asymmetrical hip belt balances the immediate access of a sling with the load stability of a traditional backpack.

Access Logic

The spiral architecture ensures that front-oriented access is an intentional, integrated function rather than an improvised feature.
